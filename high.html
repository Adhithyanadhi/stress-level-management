    SUGGESTIONS:

  • 1.CONSULT A PSYCHIATRIST:
    A Psychiatrist can help you manage stress and anxiety by finding the source or cause of your problems, as well as appropriate ways to overcome them

  • 2.ACCEPTANCE-ORIENTATED APPROACHES:
    Acceptance-oriented approaches are useful in stressful situations that you cannot control

  • 3.GET MORE SLEEP:
    A lack of sleep is a significant cause of stress. Getting enough sleep help in reducing the stress

  • 4.CUT OUT THINGS THAT ADD TO YOUR STRESS
    Sometimes, the best way to reduce your stress is to get rid of the things that are adding to your stress.

  • 5.TALK TO SOMEONE:
    Social support from friends and family can help you get through stressful times.Talk to others - preferably face to face, or at least on the phone. Share what's going on. You can get a fresh perspective while keeping your connection strong

  • 6.MEDITATION:
    Meditation brings short-term stress relief as well as lasting stress management benefits.A few minutes of practice per day can help in reducing stress.

  • 7.EXERCISE
    Try to find an exercise routine or activity you enjoy, such as walking, dancing, rock climbing or yoga
