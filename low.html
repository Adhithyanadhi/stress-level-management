• LOW LEVEL

    SUGGESTIONS:

  • 1.MEDITATION:
    Meditation brings short-term stress relief as well as lasting stress management benefits.A few minutes of practice per day can help in reducing stress.

  • 2.EXERCISE
    Try to find an exercise routine or activity you enjoy, such as walking, dancing, rock climbing or yoga

  • 3.REDUCE YOUR CAFFEINE INTAKE
    High quantities of caffeine can increase stress and anxiety

  • 4.LAUGH
    Laughing relieve your stress response and relieving tension by relaxing your muscles.It lowers cortisol, your body’s stress hormone, and boosts brain chemicals called endorphins, which help your mood

  • 5.LISTEN TO MUSIC
    Music can induce the relaxation response by helping lower blood pressure and heart rate as well as stress hormones.Listening to music you like can be a good way to relieve stress

  • 6.DEEP BREATHING
    The goal of deep breathing is to focus your awareness on your breath, making it slower and deeper.Deep breathing counters the effects of stress by slowing the heart rate and lowering blood pressure.

  • 7. SELF-HYPNOSIS
    Self-hypnosis which is very easy and can be done anywhere. One very simple technique is to focus on a word or phrase that has a positive meaning to you. Words such as "calm" "love" and "peace" work well.
